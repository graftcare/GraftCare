# Graftcare Database Structure

## Complete Table Reference

### Master Data Tables

#### 1. **VENDORS** (Suppliers)
- **Purpose:** Store supplier/vendor information
- **Fields:** id, name, contact_person, phone, gstin, dl1, dl2, address, city, pincode, bank_name, bank_acc, bank_ifsc, created_at
- **When Used:** Creating purchase invoices from suppliers
- **Example:** "Gaudev and Co", "Madhura Distributors"

#### 2. **PRODUCTS** (Medicines/Items)
- **Purpose:** Store product catalog
- **Fields:** id, name, hsn_code, pack, company, scheme, cost_price, mrp, gst_rate, created_at
- **When Used:** Adding items to any invoice (purchase, draft, proforma, final)
- **Example:** "Dolo 650", "Aspirin 500mg"

#### 3. **CUSTOMERS** (Retailers/Hospitals)
- **Purpose:** Store buyer/customer information
- **Fields:** id, name, contact_person, phone, gstin, address, city, pincode, customer_type, credit_limit, outstanding_balance, created_at
- **When Used:** Creating draft invoices for sales
- **Example:** "Apollo Hospital", "City Pharmacy Retail"
- **Customer Types:** retailer, hospital, patient

---

## Purchase Flow (Stock IN from Vendors)

#### 5. **PURCHASE_INVOICES** (Supplier Bills)
- **Purpose:** Record purchases from vendors
- **Fields:** id, vendor_id, vendor_invoice_no, invoice_date, payment_mode, amount_paid, paid_by, subtotal, total_gst, discount_amount, grand_total, payment_status, notes, created_at, updated_at
- **When Used:** You buy medicines from a supplier
- **Payment Status:** pending, partial, paid
- **Stock Effect:** ✅ **Stock INCREASES immediately** → stock_ledger entry created

#### 6. **PURCHASE_ITEMS** (Products in Purchase)
- **Purpose:** Store individual products in a purchase invoice
- **Fields:** id, purchase_invoice_id, product_id, qty, batch, expiry, mrp, buy_rate, free, discount, gst, created_at
- **When Used:** Adding each medicine to the purchase bill
- **Stock Effect:** ✅ **Stock INCREASES** by qty

---

## Sales Flow (Stock OUT to Customers)

### Stage 1: Draft (Work in Progress)

#### 7. **DRAFTS** (Customer Invoices - Editing)
- **Purpose:** Store draft customer invoices being created
- **Fields:** id, customer_id, subtotal, total_gst, discount_amount, grand_total, notes, created_at, updated_at
- **When Used:** Starting to create an invoice, want to save and edit later
- **Invoice Number:** ❌ **NONE** (no number until final invoice)
- **Stock Effect:** ❌ **NO stock change** - still editing, can modify anytime

#### 8. **DRAFT_ITEMS** (Products in Draft)
- **Purpose:** Store products added to a draft
- **Fields:** id, draft_id, product_id, batch, expiry, qty, mrp, sale_rate, discount, gst, created_at
- **When Used:** Adding medicines to draft invoice
- **Stock Effect:** ❌ **NO stock change**

---

### Stage 2: Proforma (Quote Sent to Customer)

#### 9. **PROFORMAS** (Customer Quotes - Awaiting Approval)
- **Purpose:** Store professional quotes sent to customers
- **Fields:** id, customer_id, subtotal, total_gst, discount_amount, grand_total, payment_mode, notes, created_at, updated_at
- **When Used:** Draft is ready, send as quote to customer for approval
- **Invoice Number:** ❌ **NONE** (no number yet)
- **Stock Effect:** ❌ **NO stock change** - waiting for customer approval

#### 10. **PROFORMA_ITEMS** (Products in Proforma)
- **Purpose:** Store products in the proforma quote
- **Fields:** id, proforma_id, product_id, batch, expiry, qty, mrp, sale_rate, discount, gst, created_at
- **When Used:** Converting draft → proforma (copies products from draft)
- **Stock Effect:** ❌ **NO stock change**

---

### Stage 3: Final Invoice (OFFICIAL SALE)

#### 11. **INVOICES** (Final Sales - Revenue Confirmed)
- **Purpose:** Store confirmed sales that generate revenue
- **Fields:** id, customer_id, invoice_seq (SERIAL), invoice_number (GCPS-YYYY-NNNN), invoice_date, subtotal, total_gst, discount_amount, grand_total, payment_mode, amount_received, payment_status, notes, created_at, updated_at
- **When Used:** Customer approves, proforma → final invoice (OFFICIAL SALE)
- **Invoice Number:** ✅ **AUTO-GENERATED** (GCPS-2026-0001)
  - Format: GCPS-YYYY-NNNN
  - Generated by trigger when invoice is created
  - ONLY appears in final invoices, not in drafts or proformas
- **Stock Effect:** ✅ **Stock DECREASES** → stock_ledger entry created with reason="sale"
- **Payment Status:** pending, partial, paid

#### 12. **INVOICE_ITEMS** (Products in Final Invoice)
- **Purpose:** Store confirmed products being sold
- **Fields:** id, invoice_id, product_id, batch, expiry, qty, mrp, sale_rate, discount, gst, created_at
- **When Used:** Converting proforma → invoice (copies products from proforma)
- **Stock Effect:** ✅ **Stock DECREASES** by qty

---

## Inventory Management

#### 13. **STOCK_LEDGER** (Inventory History & Tracking)
- **Purpose:** Records ALL stock movements for audit trail
- **Fields:** id, product_id, change_qty (positive/negative), reason, reference_id, notes, created_at
- **When Used:** Every purchase and every sale
- **Reasons:** 
  - "purchase" → PURCHASE_INVOICES (qty positive)
  - "sale" → INVOICES (qty negative)
  - "adjustment" → Manual stock correction
- **Used For:** 
  - Calculate current stock (SUM all change_qty for product)
  - Track expiry dates
  - Audit trail
  - Dashboard reports

## Key Rules

1. **Invoice Number Generation**
   - Only generated in INVOICES table
   - Format: GCPS-YYYY-NNNN (e.g., GCPS-2026-0001)
   - Auto-incremented via trigger
   - NOT visible in drafts or proformas

2. **Stock Management**
   - Purchase: Stock increases immediately
   - Sale: Stock decreases ONLY when proforma → invoice conversion
   - All changes recorded in stock_ledger
   - Current stock = SUM(stock_ledger.change_qty) for product

3. **Customer Tracking**
   - Search by: name, phone, customer_type, month
   - Shows all invoices for that customer
   - Filter by retailer vs hospital

4. **Data Relationships**
   - DRAFTS → PROFORMAS → INVOICES (conversion chain)
   - PURCHASE_INVOICES → STOCK_LEDGER (immediate)
   - INVOICES → STOCK_LEDGER (immediate)
   - All point to CUSTOMERS, VENDORS, PRODUCTS, STATES

## GST Management

### How It Works:
1. **User Enters GST:** Enter total GST amount → Auto-calculates SGST = GST/2, CGST = GST/2
2. **Manual Override:** User can change SGST/CGST individually anytime
3. **Recalculation:** When SGST or CGST is changed, GST updates automatically as SGST + CGST

### Form Interaction:
```
Enter GST [18]% → Auto-calculates: SGST=9%, CGST=9%
User can override any field → GST updates automatically
```
